How We Can Help

PortalGuide is an independent informational resource. We're happy to answer questions about our website content or how our guides are structured. Please review what types of inquiries we can and cannot assist with below.

✅

We Can Help With

Questions about our guide content, website topics, general information about patient portals, or feedback about our site.

❌

We Cannot Help With

Account access issues, password resets, personal health data, login errors, or anything requiring access to your actual MyChart account. For those, contact your healthcare provider directly.

📞

For Actual Portal Support

Contact your hospital or clinic's patient services team — they are the only ones who can assist with account-specific issues.

⚠️ Important: PortalGuide does not have access to any patient portal system. We cannot reset passwords, recover usernames, or view any personal information. Please contact your healthcare provider for all account-related assistance.
