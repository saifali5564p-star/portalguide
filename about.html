About PortalGuide

An independent informational resource dedicated to helping everyday users understand how patient portals work.

Our Mission

PortalGuide was created with a simple goal: to help everyday people understand how online patient portals work. Millions of people are given access to platforms like MyChart each year, but many find the sign-in process, navigation, and features confusing — especially first-time users.

We believe clear, accessible information can make a real difference. That's why we provide straightforward, plain-language guides on how MyChart and similar patient portals typically function, what to expect when accessing them, and where to go when you need real technical help.

What We Do

PortalGuide is an independent informational website. We do not provide direct access to any patient portal, and we do not handle any personal health, financial, or login information.

Our content covers topics like:

  • 🔑
    Portal Sign-In OverviewsExplaining how the MyChart login screen works and what credentials are typically used.
  • 🔄
    Account Recovery WalkthroughsDescribing the common steps portals use to help users who have forgotten their username or access details.
  • 🗺️
    Dashboard Navigation GuidesHelping users understand how the main sections of MyChart are organized.
  • 📋
    Feature ExplanationsOverviews of what's typically available inside a patient portal once a user is signed in.
  • 📞
    Official Support ReferralsGuidance on how to contact the correct support team at your healthcare provider.

Our Values

We are committed to providing accurate, neutral, and helpful information. We do not represent any healthcare system, software company, or portal provider. Our content is written to inform, not to replace official support.

We keep our language simple on purpose. Not everyone is comfortable with technology, and patient portal access shouldn't be stressful. PortalGuide exists to make things a little clearer.

Who We Are

PortalGuide is operated by a small team of web researchers and content writers who specialize in digital health literacy and patient technology guides. We have no financial relationship with MyChart, Epic Systems, or any healthcare provider.

We are not a portal support team, a call center, or a healthcare organization. If you need direct account assistance, we always encourage you to contact your healthcare provider's official support line.
